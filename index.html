About the Author

Dr Manuela Rapani, DDS, PhD

Dr Manuela Rapani graduated in Dentistry (DDS) from the University "G. d'Annunzio" of Chieti-Pescara (Italy) in 2004.

In 2009, she obtained a degree in Dental Medicine (LMD) from the University of Geneva (Switzerland) and subsequently received Swiss Federal Recognition of her qualification.

She completed a three-year specialization in Oral Surgery at the University "G. d'Annunzio" of Chieti-Pescara in 2012 and earned a PhD in Biomedical Sciences from the same university in 2016. She also completed postgraduate training in Periodontology.

Dr Rapani combines clinical experience, academic training and evidence-based education to develop innovative learning resources for dental students, postgraduate trainees and clinicians worldwide.

Her professional interests include periodontology, oral surgery, implant dentistry, digital workflows and modern educational methodologies based on active recall and evidence-based dentistry.
